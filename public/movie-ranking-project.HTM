The Best Animes According to Kurt

My Top 3 Animes of All Time

That Time I Got Reincarnated As A Slime

This is my favorite anime because of its incredible worldbuilding. The story follows Satoru Mikami, an ordinary man who is reincarnated in a fantastical world as a slime monster named Rimuru. Rimuruâ€™s journey to create a peaceful and inclusive community, along with his growth in power and influence, is captivating and beautifully executed. The rich, immersive world and the diverse cast of characters make this anime a must-watch.

Reincarnated as a Sword

This is my second favorite anime, largely due to the character Fran's bravery and courage. The story revolves around a man who is reincarnated as a powerful sword and his bond with Fran, a catgirl slave who discovers and wields him. Fran's transformation from a timid girl to a fierce warrior, especially in the fight scenes where she goes berserk, is both thrilling and inspiring. The action and character development in this anime are top-notch.

Tsukimichi: Moonlit Fantasy

A very interesting anime with a unique narrative. It follows the story of Makoto Misumi, who is transported to another world by a god only to be abandoned and left to fend for himself. Despite the harsh circumstances, Makoto uses his unique abilities and determination to carve out a place for himself in this new world. The blend of humor, action, and an unconventional storyline makes this anime stand out.
